# ChatGPT Era: Can GPT-4 perform effective Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)?
## Introduction: What is the Goals and Strategies of Exploratory Data Analysis before the GPT Era
The main job scope of an EDA involves utilizing business data to explore meaningful 
patterns and trends from the data and provide analysts with valuable insights for making strategic decisions.
To achieve their goal, they must possess a variety of skills, including SQL query writing, data cleaning and transformation, 
visualization generation, and data analysis.

EDA typically involves a variety of techniques and methods, which can be categorized into several categories listed below:
1. Descriptive Statistics:
   * __Summary Statistics__: Compute basic statistics like mean, median, mode, and standard deviation to understand the central tendency and variability of the data.
   * __Factor Analysis__: Identify underlying factors or latent variables in the dataset.
3. Data Visualization:
   * __Frequency Distributions__: Create histograms or bar charts to visualize the distribution of categorical or numerical variables.
   * __Scatter Plots__: Visualize the relationship between two numerical variables.
   * __Line Plots__: Plot time series data to observe trends and seasonal patterns.


## Exploratory Data Analysis in the GPT Era
As recent research shows [Is GPT-4 a Good Data Analyst?](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2305.15038.pdf), GPT-4 can achieve comparable performance to human data analysts at 
the evaluation of __1. correctness__ (wrong information) __2. alignment__ (analysis aligns with the question) __3. complexity__ (how in-depth is the analysis) 
__4. fluency__ (is the generated analysis fluent).

Moreover, an increasing number of user-friendly tools built upon GPT-4 are being released. For example, [Akkio](https://www.akkio.com/chat-explore) 
allows analysts and data managers to interact with their data in a natural and intuitive way. Then, [ChatGPT Code Interpreter](https://openai.com/blog/chatgpt-plugins)
allows analysts to switch from Jupyter Notebook to ChatGPT.
